How to Connect and Use Whats-Covery

  1. Grant Permissions: The app will request file access and storage permissions. Tap 'Allow' to proceed with data recovery.
  2. Scan Selection: Choose between Deep Scan, Quick Scan, or Targeted Recovery based on your needs.
  3. Scanning Process: The app will freeze all controls except Pause and Stop buttons during scanning to maintain stability.
  4. Smart Features: If a call or notification appears, the app automatically pauses. It will auto-shutdown after 15 minutes of inactivity for security.
  5. Progress Tracking: Watch the real-time percentage progress bar and scanning animation.
  6. Recovery & Export: Select recovered items and export in JSON, CSV, PDF, or XML formats.
